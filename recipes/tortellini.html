Tortellini Casserole

Description

One of my favorite dishes is Tortellini Casserole with spinach!

Now it gets delicious with the recipe Tortellini casserole with leaf spinach and melted cheese.

A pleasure for the whole family!

Ingredience

  • Noodles: Your favorite Tortellini.
  • Spinach: Frozen spinach is all you need for this dish.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan and mozzarella cheese make this casserole extra decadent.
  • Sour cream: Some nice sour cream for delicious taste.

Steps

  1. Prepare Tortellini: Prepare tortellini according to the basic recipe, add to boiling water and cook until al dente. Saut√© the chopped onion and add the milk, bring to the boil briefly.
  2. Add cheese Add the cheese, whipped cream and sour cream, stir until the cheese dissolves and season with herbs and spices.
  3. Bake: Add the frozen spinach and let it steep. Put the tortellini in a baking dish, pour the cream over it and mix it a little. Sprinkle with cheese and bake at 180 degrees for about 30 minutes.
